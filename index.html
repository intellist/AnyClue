AnyClue 2025

We help autoimmune patients understand their own body, not guess.

Target Diseases

Lupus • Crohn’s • Colitis • Hashimoto • Rheumatoid Arthritis

Problem

  • Chronic, unpredictable, and individualized diseases
  • Helplessness: "Same diet, different symptoms"
  • Poor doctor-patient feedback loop
  • Brain fog, fatigue, anxiety → need for easy tracking

Insights

  • Long-term management, not cure 🔄
  • Dietary triggers 🍽️
  • Brain fog 🧠 → Easy tracking needed
  • Personal data → insight mapping 📊
  • Empowering patients 🤝

Solution

Personalized and data-driven learning: not crowd-based advice.

Technique

Bayesian Updates:
P(symptom | food) = (α * general_data + β * user_data) / (α + β)

Temporal association problem → Multi-window analysis (0–4h, 4–8h, 8–24h)

Features

  • Food logging: photo, text, voice
  • Symptom tracking: text, voice
  • Risky score generation for food items
  • Nutrition-satisfying food combinations
  • Timeline of symptoms after risky foods
  • Closed-loop feedback system
  • Weather, sleep integration (wearables)
  • Doctor/nutritionist sharing

Personalized Bayesian Models

Building user-specific probabilistic graphs that improve over time.
